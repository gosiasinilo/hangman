Do you want to play again?

  • There is a hidden word on the screen with a dash for each letter
  • For example : _ _ _ _ _ for "apple" being a hidden word to guess.
  • You call out a letter by pressing a key button.

  • Correct guess:
  • If the letter is in the word, it will show in all the correct blank spaces.
  • Incorrect guess:
  • If the letter is not in the word, it will draw one part of the hangman figure. The hangman figure is made up of 10 parts - you have 10 incorrect guesses before the game is over.

  • Win by revealing all letters before the last image or lose if the hangman figure is completed first.
