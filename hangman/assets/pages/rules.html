How To Play

  • There is a hidden word on the screen with a dash for each letter, for example, _ _ _ _ _ for "apple"
  • You call out a letter by pressing a button on the screen
  • Correct guess: If the letter is in the word, it will show in all the correct blank spaces.
  • Incorrect guess: If the letter is not in the word, it will draw one part of the hangman figure.
  • Win by revealing all letters before the last image or lose if the hangman figure is completed first
